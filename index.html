ABOUT ME

In my everyday life I’m passionate about art design, social economics, culture structures, & social function of design. I’m into sci-fi books, healthy meatless cooking and deep analysis of mechanisms of culture.
I draw, cherish good typography and simple, but essential design solutions. I’m spending huge amount of time on analysing and being up-to date with present language of visual communication in marketing.
Basically because, I consider it universal language for present times! I admire challenges and everydaylife adventures therefore I think they are the cause for my constant progress!
